✓ Actionable Signals — Confident Across All 8 Years

Blueprint trend: Emergent & Urgent Care is the exam's fastest-growing category — from 14 questions in 2022 to 40 in both 2024 and 2025 (20% of the exam). The 8-year trend confirms this shift was building over multiple years, not a one-year anomaly. Both residents are underperforming in this category, making it the highest-leverage study target.

Body system trends (official all 8 years): Respiratory is declining. Reproductive: Female and Neurologic are rising consistently. Musculoskeletal/Injuries remains a persistent volume category. These are real trends with curriculum implications independent of blueprint classification.
